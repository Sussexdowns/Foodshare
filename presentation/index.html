Platform Capabilities

Tools for Smart Foraging

Built-in features designed to make finding, sharing, and verifying local food simple and accessible for everyone.

Interactive Map

Visualizes local food sources with category-colored pins. Users can easily scan their neighborhood for fruit trees, herbs, and vegetables.

Seasonal Filtering

Features an In Season Now filter that dynamically updates based on the time of year, preventing wasted trips.

Precision Search

Integrates what3words and address search to pinpoint exact locations of wild food, often hidden off main paths.

Community Trust

Robust reporting and rating tools allow the community to flag issues, update statuses, and keep the map data accurate and safe.

CORE PRINCIPLES

Built on Trust & Safety

We combine robust technical security with community-focused ethical guidelines to ensure a safe, reliable platform for everyone.

Verified Identity

Secure email verification with mandatory verification ensures real users, reduces spam, and builds a foundation of accountability.

Admin Governance

Strict role-based permissions mean only trusted community moderators can approve new locations and sensitive items.

Ethical Foraging

Built-in UI prompts remind users to seek landowner permission, respect nature, and harvest sustainably without over-picking.

Community Watch

One-click reporting mechanisms allow the community to instantly flag inaccurate data or unsafe locations for admin review.
